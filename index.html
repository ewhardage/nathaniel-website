Our Philosophy

Care That Starts
with You

At Painted Skies Chiropractic, our philosophy centers on the nervous system as the foundation of overall health. When it's clear and communicating well, the body can adapt, recover, and thrive. Chiropractic care isn't about chasing symptoms. It's about restoring balance so your body can function as it was meant to.

Nervous System First

We treat your nervous system as the root of your overall health, not just your symptoms. When communication flows freely through your spine, your body can heal and thrive naturally.

Personalized Approach

We take a thoughtful, individualized approach to care, meeting you where you are and listening to your story. Because no two bodies are the same, every path to healing is unique.

A Welcoming Environment

Our goal is to create a calm, welcoming space where you feel supported, understood, and empowered in your health journey, like coming home to someone who genuinely cares.

What We Offer

Our Services

From hands-on adjusting to advanced therapeutic techniques, we offer a full range of chiropractic services, all tailored to your specific needs and goals.

01

Ultrasound Therapy

Ultrasound therapy uses sound waves to promote deep tissue healing, reduce inflammation, and improve circulation. This treatment is often used to relieve pain, decrease muscle stiffness, and accelerate recovery from soft-tissue injuries.

02

Electrical Stimulation (E-Stim)

E-stim delivers gentle electrical impulses to targeted muscles and nerves to reduce pain, improve muscle activation, and support healing. It's commonly used for pain management, muscle re-education, and reducing muscle spasms.

03

Manual Adjusting

Manual adjusting is a hands on technique that uses precise, controlled movements to restore joint mobility, improve alignment, and relieve discomfort. This approach helps enhance movement, reduce tension, and support overall musculoskeletal health.

04

Instrument Assisted Adjusting

Instrument assisted adjusting uses specialized handheld tools to deliver precise, low-force adjustments. This technique allows for accurate targeting of problem areas while minimizing strain, making it a comfortable option for patients who prefer a gentler approach.

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Nathaniel Flores

D.C., Doctor of Chiropractic

My name is Dr. Nathaniel Flores and I am a chiropractor proudly serving the Portales and Eastern New Mexico region. Raised in Melrose and deeply rooted in the Eastern New Mexico region, I focus on bringing a strong commitment to caring for the people and families who helped shape my life. My journey into chiropractic care began while working as a Chiropractic Assistant, where I witnessed firsthand how conservative, hands-on care could help patients move, feel, and live better, an experience that inspired me to pursue my Doctor of Chiropractic degree from Cleveland University Kansas City.

I believe great care starts with connection. I take a personable, relationship based approach to treatment, focusing on communication, patient comfort, and individualized care tailored to each person's goals and lifestyle. I value collaborative care and strive to create a welcoming environment where patients feel respected, heard, and supported.

The name Painted Skies Chiropractic is inspired by the unforgettable sunsets of Eastern New Mexico, a symbol in my life that reminds me of home, community, and gratitude. I spend most of my free time hunting, playing pickle ball, golfing, and being active in the gym where I seek to promote a healthy lifestyle through example. I am eager to treat those of the surrounding area of all ages and cannot wait to become more involved in the communities that help shape who I am.
